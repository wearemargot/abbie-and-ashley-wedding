Information

Here's some helpful information to help make the day run smoothly, help you save some money, and a few recommendations from us.

Parking

at CitiPark

Parking is available at CitiPark at Leeds Dock. The car park is ideally located for accomodation at Holiday Inn Express, the bus stop to Hyde Park Picture House and Fearns.

Prices

Guests can purchase a weekend ticket at the car park for £20. This is valid from midday Friday to midday on Monday and allows 24 hour entry and exit.

We can also offer guests a discount of £7 a day on prebooked parking. This option is limited to single entry/exit per day. Each day's parking must be booked seperately.

To prebook parking, go to the CitiPark booking site and use discount code LDOCK7.

Travel

by public transport

Leeds Station is around a 20 minute walk from Leeds Dock.

You can also use the Leeds Dock Water Taxi (which we like to call the "Egg Boats") from the South entrance of Leeds Station to Leeds Dock. They run about every 15 minutes and cost £3 per person.

Accomodation

Holiday Inn Express at Leeds Dock is the hotel with best access to parking, the bus to Hyde Park Picture House for the ceremony and Fearns.

Leonardo Hotel at Brewery Wharf is also within walking distance of Leeds Dock.

Gifts

Please do not feel that a gift is necessary, your presence is all we need. However, if you would really like to give us something, a contribution towards our honeymoon would really make our day.

Recommendations

for breakfast and stuff to do nearby

We live right by Leeds Dock, so naturally have some recommendations of lovely things to eat and drink there, and things to do in the local area.

Breakfast and coffee at Leeds Dock

Most days we get coffee from North Star Coffee Shop, who also have a great food menu and coffee beans to take home.

We also love to get a coffee from Vinnie at his coffee stand, Coffee Synthesis, near the entrance to CitiPark and take a walk down the river.

For the best baked goods around, NOVA Bakehouse is a must. We love to share their cinammon buns, cardamon buns, morning buns and something unique to them - morning cakes.

A little further afield

If you're spending a little time in Leeds here's some other things we love.

Coffee

Our favourite places for coffee are from Lee at Miles and Co. in the beautiful Kirkgate Market and Laynes.

Food

Some of our favourite places to eat are House of Fu, Ox Club, Rudys Pizza, Sarto and a cheeky burger from Slap and Pickle or shawarma from Sqew.

Excursions

From Leeds, Yorkshire is your oyster! But, some particular spots we love are:

  • The Hepworth in Wakefield for it's perminant Barbara Hepworth exhibition, other exhibitions and brilliant food and coffee from Carl, Vic and others in the cafe
  • Yorkshire Sculpture Park is a brilliant day out with world class sculptures (our favourites are the Barbara Hepworth, Henry Moore and Ai Wei Wei's), nice walks and food from The Weston
  • Salts Mill in the beautiful UNESCO World Heritage village of Saltaire. We love to go and see the perminant David Hockney exhibitions, the wonderful book and home shop and food from Salt's Diner

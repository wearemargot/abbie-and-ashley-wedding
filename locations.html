Locations

Our wedding will be taking place in 2 locations.

The ceremony will take place at Hyde Park Picture House, the world's last remaining gaslit cinema, first built in 1914, and where we had our first date.

We'll be hosting guests for a meal, and an evening of fun and dancing at Fearns at Leeds Dock, just around the corner from our home.

Parking and accomodation will be available at Leeds Dock.

Guests will be transported between venues on a 1950s open top bus - don't forget your ticket!
